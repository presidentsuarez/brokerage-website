An asset you can sell when you're done.

Financial advisors, insurance agents, accountants — they can all sell their book of business at retirement. Real estate never could. The Book changes that. Each year you produce, a percentage of your GCI accrues into your Book, the group matches a share, it vests over five years — and when you're ready to step back, you cash it out or sell your client base to a younger agent in your pod, financed through us.
